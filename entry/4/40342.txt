           NU(En)=NUtherm+ALPHA*(En-dEk)+dNUj, where              40342  4    8 
           NUthermal - average number of prompt neutrons at Pu-24140342  4    9 
           thermal fission;                                       40342  4   10 
           dEk - the change in fragment kinetic energy not        40342  4   11 
           associated with a change in fragment yields;           40342  4   12 
           dNUj - the change in the number of prompt neutrons     40342  4   13 
           resulting only from difference in fragment yields for  40342  4   14 
           neutrons of energy En and for thermal neurons.         40342  4   15 
           dEk and dNUj were determined from exp. measurement of  40342  4   16 
           fragment kinetic energies and yields.                  40342  4   17 
           ALPHA - coefficient determined using the exp.data of   40342  4   18 
           this work and data of other authors; it was assumed    40342  4   19 
           that ALPHA increases linearly from 0.094 1/MeV for     40342  4   20 
           thermal neutron fission to 0.120 1/MeV fro 5 MeV       40342  4   21 
           neutrons.                                              40342  4   22
