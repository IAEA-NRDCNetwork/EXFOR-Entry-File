METHOD     (TOF) Multidimensional measurements involving a        41516  1   27 
           determination of the times of flight of a neutron and  41516  1   28 
           two fragments along with the angle between the velocity41516  1   29 
           vectors of neutron and light fragment.                 41516  1   30 
           (PSD) Double discrimination by the pulse shape and     41516  1   31 
           time-of-flight was used to separate the events produced41516  1   32 
           by neutrons and gammas.                                41516  1   33 
           (COINC) Each of stop MWPDs .                           41516  1   34 
            10 weekly cycles of measurement were performed.       41516  1   35 
DETECTOR   (MWPC)  One MWPD as start detector, located within     41516  1   36 
            7mm range from target parallel to target plane.       41516  1   37 
            Eight pairs of multiwire proportional fission-fragment41516  1   38 
            detectors, operating voltage 400-500 V, placed in two 41516  1   39 
            arching formations diametrically opposed each other in41516  1   40 
            the reaction chamber at distance of 140mm from the    41516  1   41 
            chamber axis.                                         41516  1   42 
           (SCIN) Two scintillation detectors for neutron         41516  1   43 
            registration - stilbene crystals of 50 mm diameter,   41516  1   44 
            50mm high and 60 mm diameter, 40mm high mounted on    41516  1   45 
            Hamamatsu-R6091 phototubes, positioned with 90 deg    41516  1   46 
            angle between their respective axis at distance about 41516  1   47 
            50 cm from target. Cylindrical shield made of 30 mm   41516  1   48 
            thick layer of lead and 40mm thick layer of           41516  1   49 
            polyethylene. Neutron registration threshold          41516  1   50 
            150-200 keV. Position of neutron detectors was fixed. 41516  1   51 
            Total counting rate was about 100 events/min for both 41516  1   52 
            neutron detectors. 2E+06 events were accumulated.     41516  1   53 
SAMPLE      U-235 target, enriched to 99.99% purity, consisted of 41516  1   54 
            280 microg/cm**2 thick U-235 layer sprayed as UF4 on  41516  1   55 
            self-supporting 70 microg/cm**2 thick Ti backing      41516  1   56 
            making a circular spot of 15 mm diameter.             41516  1   57
