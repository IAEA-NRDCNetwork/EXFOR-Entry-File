           are detected in the ten-channel ladder. The relative   L0281001   21 
           efficiencies of the different channels were measured   L0281001   22 
           as usual by using a carbon target to provide a Rat     L0281001   23 
           inelastic continuum over the energy interval           L0281001   24 
           corresponding to the ten channels. As is usual with    L0281001   25 
           such measurements, four or five energy settings of     L0281001   26 
           the spectrometer are employed so that the peak         L0281001   27 
           appears in a different channel in each setting. The    L0281001   28 
           results are then combined by the computer program. In  L0281001   29 
           this way the effects of changing relative              L0281001   30 
           efficiencies can be averaged out.                      L0281001   31
