METHOD     (GSPEC,EXTB,ACTIV) Liquid argon was bombarded with     C196200100015 
           310-, 425-, and 578-MeV protons. Immediately after     C196200100016 
           each irradiation, the liquid argon was transferred     C196200100017 
           from the "irradiation" Dewar to a precooled            C196200100018 
           "counting" Dewar and counted at varying distances up   C196200100019 
           to 12 in. from a 3.6-cm3 Ge(Li) diode gamma-ray        C196200100020 
           spectrometer. Pulses were stored in a Nuclear Data     C196200100021 
           multichannel analyzer, utilizing 1024 channels         C196200100022 
           calibrated at either 3 or 6 keV per channel.           C196200100023 
           Successive counting periods of 20 to 8000 sec were     C196200100024 
           taken for several hours after each irradiation so      C196200100025 
           that decay curves were obtained for use in the data    C196200100026 
           analysis. The counting efficiency as a function of     C196200100027 
           gamma-ray energy deposited in the diode was obtained   C196200100028 
           by calibration of known radionuclide sources which     C196200100029 
           were standardized against IAEA standards. The          C196200100030 
           "irradiation" Dewar was swabbed with alcohol and dry   C196200100031 
           tissues which were counted to determine what, if any,  C196200100032 
           quantity of spallation products had adhered to the     C196200100033 
           sides of the Dewar. The empty, wiped Dewar was also    C196200100034 
           counted to ensure that no spallation products          C196200100035 
           remained. The collection efficiency of the swabs for   C196200100036 
           the long-lived radioisotopes 24Na, 22Na, and 7Be was   C196200100037 
           found to be greater than 98% based on a comparison of  C196200100038 
           the activity of 24Na measured in the Dewar containing  C196200100039 
           the argon and in the swabs.                            C196200100040
