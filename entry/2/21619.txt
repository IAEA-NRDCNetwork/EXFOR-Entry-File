METHOD     (TOF) Time-of-flight. Cross sections are               21619  1   29 
            derived from the capture gamma-ray spectra,           21619  1   30 
            recorded as a function of the time-of-flight.         21619  1   31 
            The spectra are recorded in anti-Compton and          21619  1   32 
            single escape modes simultaneously.                   21619  1   33

            Na-I-(Tl) Crystal ( 10 % resolution for               21619  1   35 
            the 662 keV Cs-137 gamma line) centered in an         21619  1   36 
            annular Na-I-(Tl)-crystal, 304 mm long, outer         21619  1   37 
            diam 279 mm, inner diam 89 mm, (30 %                  21619  1   38 
            resolution for 511 keV gamma line of Na-24).          21619  1   39 
            These detectors are used in anti-Compton-mode         21619  1   40 
            and in single-escape-mode simultaneously.             21619  1   41 
            Protected by boron acid , lead, paraffin+ CO3Li2 .    21619  1   42 
           (SCIN ) Plastic scintillator for neutron flux          21619  1   43 
            determination.                                        21619  1   44 
           (LONGC) Calibrated directional long counter            21619  1   45 
            for neutron flux determination.                       21619  1   46
