           (GSPEC) Isotopic fission yields were determined by     2315700100045 
           gamma spectrometry. The beta-decay of the fission      2315700100046 
           products occurs after their flight path in the         2315700100047 
           spectrometer (about 1 micros). The fission products are2315700100048 
           then implanted in a movable tape, the position of which2315700100049 
           is fixed for each single gamma measurement (about 30   2315700100050

           of the tape to measure the gamma-decay.                2315700100052

           loss dE, deposited in the first part of the chamber,   2315700100054 
           as well as the total energy E deposited in the whole   2315700100055 
           chamber. Ionization chamber alone is used for the mass 2315700100056 
           yield measurement.                                     2315700100057 
           (HPGE) Setup with two HPGe clovers -  for the isotopic 2315700100058 
           yields determination.                                  2315700100059
