           technique, at 20 degrees in the lab. system , using    2312900100134 
           5-mm thick CH2 target.                                 2312900100135 
           (TOF) TOF - to select peak neutrons and reject tail    2312900100136 
           neutrons.                                              2312900100137 
           TOF of each CP from reaction target at the Medley      2312900100138 
           centre to first SIBAR is subtracted from the total TOF.2312900100139 
           (EDE) DeltaE-E technique for particle identification   2312900100140 
           and energy measurement.                                2312900100141 
           Instrumental background was measured without sample.   2312900100142

           system, measured from CH2 target with 175 MeV QMN,     2312900100144

ANALYSIS   Absolute double-differential cross-sections SIG are    2312900100195 
           obtained from net counts N, applying the expression 3.52312900100196

           SIG/N=(SIG-H/I-H)*(2A/ACH2)*(mCH2/m)*(FCH2/F)*         2312900100198 
                 (OmegaCH2/Omega)*(1/deltaE)                      2312900100199 
           where A and ACH2 are respectively the atomic weight of 2312900100200 
           the reaction target and of the CH2 target,             2312900100201 
           m and mCH2 are the target masses,                      2312900100202 
           F and FCH2 are the neutron fluences measured with one  2312900100203 
           of the neutron monitors, OmegaCH2/Omega is the ratio   2312900100204 
           between the solid angle seen by the telescope at 20    2312900100205 
           degrees and the telescope used for the measurement,    2312900100206 
           deltaE is the energy bin width,                        2312900100207

           peak provides the measured number of protons induced   2312900100209
