DETECTOR   (HPGE) Activities of the gold foils were measured with 2316600100049 
           a well calibrated, passively shielded 76 cm3 HPGe      2316600100050 
           detector, with 1.7 keV resolution at 1.33 MeV gamma-ray2316600100051 
           energy and a relative efficiency of 30%.               2316600100052 
             Zn activities were counted with two clover-type HPGe 2316600100053 
           detectors facing each other in close geometry. Each    2316600100054 
           clover detector (Eurisys Measures) consists of four    2316600100055 
           independent n-type Ge crystals in a four-leaf-clover   2316600100056 
           arrangement with 0.2mm gaps in between. The originally 2316600100057 
           cylindrical crystals of 50 mm diameter, 70 mm length;  2316600100058 
           active volume of about 145 cm3 per crystal.            2316600100059 
           (GLASD) Neutron flux was continuously monitored and    2316600100060 
           recorded in time steps of typically 60 s by means of   2316600100061 
           6Li-glass detector at 1 m distance from the target.    2316600100062 
           The individual Ge crystals have resolution of typically2316600100063 
           2.0 keV at 1.33 MeV. At 122 keV, the peak-to-total     2316600100064 
           ratio is about 45 . The efficiency of the clover setup 2316600100065 
           was determined with a set of pointlike calibration     2316600100066 
           sources (22Na, 54Mn, 60Co, 65Zn, 88Y,131Ba, and 137Cs) 2316600100067
