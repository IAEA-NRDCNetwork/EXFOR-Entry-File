              a full R-matrix fit of the capture yields with the  2302700100138 
              SAMMY code using the Reich-Moore formalism and      2302700100139 
           including corrections for Doppler and resolution       2302700100140 
           broadening and for sample multiple scattering and      2302700100141 
           self-shielding. By means of statistical methods it was 2302700100142 
           verified that all observed resonances can be           2302700100143 
           confidently assumed to be s wave.                      2302700100144 
           It was found that the first few resonances of Os-186   2302700100145 
           and Os-187 could not be fitted because of sample       2302700100146

           shapes. The effect of sample inhomogeneities could not 2302700100148
