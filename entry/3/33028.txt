ANALYSIS   (AREA) The photo peak areas of different gamma rays    3302800100030 
           were calculated by subtracting the linear Compton      3302800100031

           monitor were determined using decay equations. The     3302800100035 
           independent yields were determined from a knowledge    3302800100036 
           of separation time activities and employing usual      3302800100037 
           decay-growth equations after correcting for the        3302800100038 
           precursor contribution. The cumulative yields of       3302800100039 
           fission products in the thermal neutron-induced        3302800100040 
           fission of 229-Th, 241-Pu and 245-Cm as well as in     3302800100041 
           spontaneous fission of 252-Cf were converted to their  3302800100042 
           fraction yields using the mass chain yields.The        3302800100043 
           fractional cumulative yields (FCY) of a few fission    3302800100044 
           products were determined from the activities of the    3302800100045 
           daughter products using a parent-daughter genetic      3302800100046 
           equation.                                              3302800100047
