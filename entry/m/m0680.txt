            pulsed photons and a  4PI  time  projection  chamber  M068000100030

            event-by-event mode.  The photon beam  was  produced  M068000100032 
            via the Compton backscattering of laser photons with  M068000100033 
            high-energy   electrons.    The    4He(g,p)3H    and  M068000100034 
            4He(g,n)3He  reaction  cross sections were found out  M068000100035 
            to increase monotonically with  energy  up  to  29.8  M068000100036 
            MeV,   in   contrast  to  the  result  of  a  recent  M068000100037 
            theoretical  calculation  based   on   the   Lorentz  M068000100038 
            integral   transform   method   that   predicted   a  M068000100039 
            pronounced peak at around 26 - 27  MeV.  The  energy  M068000100040 
            dependence  of  the  obtained  4He(g,n)3He  reaction  M068000100041 
            cross  section  up  to  26.5   MeV   is   marginally  M068000100042 
            consistent    with    a   Faddeev-type   calculation  M068000100043
