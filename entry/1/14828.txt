METHOD     (OLMS,COINC) The primary method A used in this work    1482800100016 
           is a direct technique based on simultaneous            1482800100017 
           determination of the total number of neutrons and the  1482800100018 
           total number of precursor nuclei from mass separated   1482800100019 
           samples. The absolute value of Pn depends critically   1482800100020 
           on the neutron counter efficiency and the ion          1482800100021 
           counting efficiency. A secondary method B of           1482800100022 
           determining Pn was also employed in some of the        1482800100023 
           experiments. This method was based on an equivalent    1482800100024 
           definition of Pn as the ratio of saturation neutron    1482800100025 
           activity Asat(n) to saturation beta activity           1482800100026 
           Asat(beta).                                            1482800100027 
ANALYSIS   (DECAY) For method B, the delayed-neutron decay        1482800100028 
           curves were analyzed by a least squares computer code  1482800100029 
           CLSQ into to components - one with the half-life of    1482800100030 
           the delayed-neutron precursor and one with an          1482800100031 
           infinite half-life for the background. Delayed-        1482800100032 
           neutron emission probabilities (%) from SOLAR          1482800100033 
           experiments were based on weighted average of methods  1482800100034 
           A and B.                                               1482800100035

           the pulsed beam experiments so it is necessary to      1482800100044
