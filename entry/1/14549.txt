SAMPLE     The two sources consisted of 20 and 12 mu-g of 252Cf,  14549  1    9 
           deposited on platinum backings and having a nominal    14549  1   10

           The detectors were energy-calibrated by comparing a    14549  1   23

METHOD     (COINC) Three methods of taking data were used in the  14549  1   34 
           course of this experiment:                             14549  1   35

           Mode 2: To collect the energy spectra of the more      14549  1   46
